About Me

A Michigander at heart. Born in Detroit and raised in the Metropolitan Area. I graduated from Michigan State University(GO GREEN!) with a degree in Advertising. Shortly after graduation, I moved to Washington D.C to pursue a career in creative advertising. However, life seldom goes the way you plan. I actually found myself drawn towards programming and more specifically web development. I then decided to pursue my Masters degree in Computer Science and graduated from the University of DC. More importantly, my wife said "yes" and I was blessed with all my amazing children.

Projects

Front-End development is my passion and my primary focus. Most of my projects involve html, css and Javascript. This includes leveraging libraries such as jQuery, Datatables, D3, and Highcharts. On top of that, I also use frameworks such as Bootstrap, Skeleton and Angular 1.x. Although I do have experience with backend technologies such as the .Net Framework, C# and PHP.
